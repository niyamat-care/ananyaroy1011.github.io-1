I am grateful to have co-founded Niyamat, India's first digital wellbeing initiative recognized by the World Economic Forum's Global Shapers, the Unilever Global Wellbeing Program, The Children's Screentime Action Network, Fairplay, The Harvard Resolution Project, United Nations LookUp Live and more. From encouraging digital and mental wellbeing through art, music, storytelling and trivia to conducting mindfulness sessions with children's non profit organizations across India, it is our mission to de-stigmatize therapy and make mental health accessible to students and young professionals across the country.
