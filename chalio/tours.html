Chalio! is a next generation travel app that answers the question where would a local go within a 15 minute walking distance of your current location.

The WHO, WHAT & WHY…

We focus on the walking distance because it's our belief that to truly immerse yourself in a city you have to experience it just as those who live there, one step at a time. Using data sourced from residents Chalio! curates category results to give users the perspective of individuals who really know a city and its businesses.

Targeted to major metropolitan areas the app will unearth the best restaurants, bars and coffee spots and weigh the current walking distance for a user.

An Avid Traveller or Local Connoisseur?

When visiting a new city or walking around your hometown users can select from our static categories (feed me, need a drink, or grab a coffee), custom city categories or let Chalio! make a single recommendation and send you there with “Walk Me To It”.

A streamlined description page features Photos, descriptions, directions, POVs, hours, website link and the ability to share a place on your preferred social platform - all right from a business description page.

Chalio! puts key features in the hands of its users. These include:

  • Localized search metric - makes Chalio! the richest database of local places.
  • The ability to give your Point of View (POV) - sharing your experience allows consumers to swap stories about a particular place and capture the essence of a business. For visitors, seeing these local POVs allows for never before access into local city life.
  • The ability to add places you previously lived, which will expand your local footprint.

Own a business?

Start a conversation with your customers. Owners will be able to control the images, hours, descriptions, and social media links giving businesses the opportunity to interact with app users by responding to feedback.
